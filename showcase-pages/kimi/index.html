My favorite movie

Kiminonawa

Also known as "Your Name", Kiminonawa is an anime movie that has amazing visuals, a beautiful story, and a creative plot.

Meet the main characters!

Taki is a high school boy in Tokyo. He is a talented sketch artist and has aspirations to be an architect
Mitsuha is a high school girl dissatisfied with her life in Itomori, a mountainous and rural town of Gifu Prefecture.
